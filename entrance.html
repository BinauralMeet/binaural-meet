Usage

Mute/unmute and device selection

There are icons at the bottom left corner.

If you use a headphone, click the single speaker icon on the far left and change to headphone mode.

Edit avatar information

At the beginning, your avatar is located the center of the map and it has a config menu.
Click the red dots and open the config dialog.
Then, input your info and click "Save" to save it for this session. or "Save to browser" to save it forever.

Upload image

Click the bottom far right icon and open share dialog.

Then, click "Image".

Then, upload an image file and click "Done" button.

The image will appear on the map. It may take 10 to 20 seconds.
Then adjust location by dragging the image and click the "Share" button on the top left of the image to share the image to all participants.
Once shared image is pinned to the map. You need to unpin it from the title bar of the image.

Share other thing

You can also share your mouse cursor, text labels and iframe (web site which allows to display in iframe). Only one user can share screen.
