Usage

Unmute mic to speak

To speak, use headphones and click . If you don't have headphone and use loudspeaker, click and change to to enable echo canceller. Otherwise howling will occur. For sound localization and distance based volume change, stereo headphones are needed.

Mute/unmute and device selection

There are icons at the bottom left corner.

If you use a headphone, click the single speaker icon on the far left and change to headphone mode.

Share contents

By dropping on map or paste by keyboard operation, you can share images, text, YouTube videos, URLs which can be shown in an <iframe> element.
You can also share screen and mouse by clicking the share icon and open the share dialog.
Screens can be shared as content or main screen but only one user can use main screen.

Edit avatar information

At the beginning, your avatar is located the center of the map and it has a config menu.
Click the red dots and open the config dialog.
Then, input your info and click "Save" to save it for this session. or "Save to browser" to save it forever.
